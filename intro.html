Ruthwell Cross

A large version of The Ruthwell Cross is hosted by ISTI CNR through 3DHOP viewer. This model is intended for use by students, teachers, and members of the public who are interested in exploring the cross and learning more about it. Should you encounter any bugs, glitches, lack of functionality, or other problems on the website, please let us know immediately by writing an email to jocelyn.mcknight@uleth.ca. Your help in this regard is greatly appreciated.
